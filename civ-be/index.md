## 🏛️ Sponsor Victories
Below are the specific sponsors and their traits required for completion.

| Sponsor | Leader | Specialty Highlights |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Al Falah** | Arshia Kishk | Increased yields from City Developments. |
| **Pan-Asian Cooperative** | Daoming Sochua | Wonder production bonus and free Wonder per city. |
| **People's African Union** | Elodie | Faster Virtue acquisition through Culture. |
| **INTEGR** | Lena Ebner | Significant Diplomatic Capital discounts. |
| **Kavithan Protectorate** | Kavitha Thakur | Rapid tile acquisition and reduced plot costs. |
| **People's African Union** | Samatar Jama Barre | Food growth bonuses and Specialist yields. |
| **Slavic Federation** | Vadim Koslov | Orbital unit longevity and strategic resource boosts. |

### Detailed Sponsor Traits

#### **Al Falah (Arshia Kishk)**
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: +150% Yield from City Developments.
    * Level 2: +175% Yield from City Developments.
    * Level 3: +200% Yield from City Developments.

#### **Pan-Asian Cooperative (Daoming Sochua)**
* **Specialty:** +10% Production towards Wonders, +25% Worker speed.
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: First Wonder in every city is free; +1 Diplomatic Capital from Wonders.
    * Level 2: First Wonder in every city is free; +2 Diplomatic Capital from Wonders.
    * Level 3: First Wonder in every city is free; +3 Diplomatic Capital from Wonders.

#### **People's African Union (Elodie)**
* **Specialty:** Free Virtue for every 10 earned.
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: Free Virtue every 10 earned.
    * Level 2: Free Virtue every 9 earned.
    * Level 3: Free Virtue every 8 earned.

#### **INTEGR (Lena Ebner)**
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: 50% less DC for Agreements; 25% less DC for Purchases.
    * Level 2: 60% less DC for Agreements; 50% less DC for Purchases.
    * Level 3: 75% less DC for Agreements; 75% less DC for Purchases.

#### **Kavithan Protectorate (Kavitha Thakur)**
* **Specialty:** Tiles acquired twice as fast.
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: 30% reduction in plot costs.
    * Level 2: 40% reduction in plot costs.
    * Level 3: 50% reduction in plot costs.

#### **People's African Union (Samatar Jama Barre)**
* **Specialty:** +10% Food in growing cities; starts with Old Earth Relic.
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: +10 Food Growth; Specialists +1 extra yield.
    * Level 2: +15 Food Growth; Specialists +1 extra yield.
    * Level 3: +25 Food Growth; Specialists +2 extra yield.

#### **Slavic Federation (Vadim Koslov)**
* **Specialty:** Orbital units stay 50% longer; +50% Petroleum.
* **Trait Levels:**
    * Level 1: Orbital resource costs -1; start with 4 Petroleum/Titanium/Geothermal.
    * Level 2: Gain 2 Petroleum, Titanium, and Geothermal.
    * Level 3: Orbital resource costs -2.
